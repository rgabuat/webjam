WHAT IS TECHNOFEST

TECHNOFEST: Technology Festival
is organized by FEU Institute of Technology Group of Schools - FEU Alabang, FEU Diliman and FEU Tech. It is a convention that celebrates the bounty of technology that this generation has to offer to its people. Esteemed guests and speakers are invited to talk for FEU Tech and its delegates from different colleges and universities on matters like challenges and technology fallbacks. Delegates from other colleges and universities could also join competitions and paper presentations related to their degree and program. This program will celebrate technology through boosting its participants’ enthusiasm that shall trigger creativity and innovation through talks, workshops, presentations and competitions and make a mark for the centuries to come as we harvest bountiful competence and innovative technologies.
